Jesus was to parallel Moses to prove he is God.
That he was telling the truth.
That Jesus is God in the flesh (mortal like us).

- Moses a jew was born when Egyptions (gentiles) ruled over the people of Israel

- Jesus a jew was born when Romans (gentiles) ruled over the people of Israel.

- The Pharaoh during Moses' time decreed that all male Hebrew babies should be killed.
and they were.

- King Herod during Jesus' time decreed that all male Hebrew babies should be killed.
and the were.

- Moses' name means to draw out of water. The Pharaoh's daughter named him
that after she rescued him out of the nile river giving him salvation; meaning
(preservation or deliverance from harm, ruin, or loss.).

- Well Jesus' name in ancient hebrew is 'Yeshua' which means salvation.

- God favored Moses as an infant. Pharaoh's daughter took him from the water
and he became prince the of Egypt.

- Well with Jesus, when he was born, Wise men worshipped Him and presented
very wealthy riches to him, (Gold, Frankincense, and Myrrh. Very expensive ).

- Moses was brought up by Pharaoh who was not his real father.

- Jesus was brought up by Joseph who was not his real father.

- Moses saw the burdens of his Israelite people and felt great compassion
on them.

- Jesus saw His people as sheep (jews and gentiles) without a shepherd
(no guidance) and had great compassion on them.

- Moses went to a well, was kind to the daughters of the priest of Midian,
and watered their livestock (not the usual custom).

- Jesus went to a well, was kind to a Samaritan woman and offered her
water (not the usual custom).

- Moses was in exile in a foreign land until the king of Egypt died.

- Jesus was in exile in a foreign land until King Herod died

You see. The list can go on and on. Everything Moses did, Jesus had to do.
Moses was used to represent God before his coming to earth.
Just like how Moses collected his people and brought them out of uncertainty,
Jesus collected his people and brought them out of uncertainty.
Moses went up the mountain of Mount Sinai to speak with God and recieve his orders
for earth. He left his people at the bottom for 40 whole days and nights.
Many of the people starting turning away from Moses. Thinking he abandoned
them. So when he came back down off the deserted mountain top, many followers
fell away.

Then 1443 years later, Jesus was born and spread his message of salvation
to his people. He said he would be leaving them (dying) but will be coming back soon.
And look what is happening today. People are asking, Where is He? He said He
would be back soon? Now many are falling away just as what happened with Moses.

To believe that he is the first and the last. The alpha and omega.
The beginning and the end.
