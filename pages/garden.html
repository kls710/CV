What was it like and what happened to it?

First lets interpret the words "Garden and Eden".
The ancient Greek translation is the words gan and `Eden.
gan meaning: enclosed garden, and `Eden meaning: pleasure.
So the Garden of Eden was an enclosed garden that fulfilled pleasure by its beauty.
This is referring to The Earth as a garden that was also beautiful and caused pleasure
for your soul.

God created an absolute paradise. The greenest most lush herbs, plants, and trees
you could ever dream up. Water so pure, you could reach out and drink from
any stream. The most pure air you could ever imagine.
Imagine earth from the beginning. No humans, no cut down trees, absolute zero pollution.
The most pure bliss you could ever imagine.

God wanted to create us in his image, and wanted man to use his free will
to choose Him. When God made Adam and Eve, He placed them in the Garden.
There were two kinds of trees. One of good and evil, the other, the tree
of life. The tree of good and evil represented satan, the tree of life
represented God.

To better illustrate this. Look at a mother with her son. Her son picks up
a bottle of cleaner. The mother tells him to put it down, do not drink from this.
She issues a loving warning, do not drink from this because you will be poisoned.
If the child disobeys and drinks from the bottole, he will be poisoned,
which is a huge problem.

This is how God operates. He told Adam and Eve, of all the trees in the garden
do not eat of this tree. Then gives them the free will
because he never wants to force anyones love from them but he is our Father
and will punish us just like our human parents would for disobedience.

God was honest about the tree of good and evil. He warned them that eating
from it would result in death. But he left the choice up to them.
Then satan twisted God's words to Eve and lied to her. Making her believe that
God was withholding something incredible from them. Satan made her doubt
God's good heart for them.

Once satan was able to convince them to eat from the tree, the poison of
sin entered their lives. Meaning: Now they know what its like to do what
they were told not to. The consequences of what they did were so profound,
affecting all of humanity even until today.

That is why God made plans, took steps to try and save us from the effects of
what happened in the Garden of Eden. He wants us to come back to him. To the light.
