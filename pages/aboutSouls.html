Your Spirit described.

I will mention -for education sake; that the word Spirit has been
translated from both ancient Hebrew and Greek. So as in the Bible,
when you see the word, breath and life in the Bible. It is referring
to a 'soul' not your 'spirit'.

The differences between Spirit and Soul are pronounced in the Bible.
A soul is referenced as a living thing. Like me saying, "oh, that poor soul"
moreless "oh that poor person".

A Spirit on the other hand is the flow of energy inside of you. It is what makes
you, you. What gives you 'life'.

Let me put it this way.

Think of your Soul as a battery powered radio. And your Spirit is the battery.
The battery powers the radio bringing it to life. Giving it a 'life-force'.
Without your spirit -the battery, your Soul -Your flesh and bones, will die.
But like any radio, it can only run so long before mechanical failures happen.
Your battery -Spirit, still has plenty of juice, but your Soul -the body
withers away.

This is why God says that when he returns back to earth. He will not only
call those of us that are still alive to meet him in the air, but during
the trumpet blasts of his coming, he will raise the dust from our dead bodies
back up and place our spirits into transformed souls -bodies.

They will be angelic and last for all of eternity. So long as your spirit
is saved that is...
