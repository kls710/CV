What is prayer?


Prayer is a way for us to communicate our thoughts, needs,
and desires to Him. It is an open source way that God created for a
believer to make known the deepest feelings in our heart.


Asking God for things.

You are to petition, asking God for things, Jesus said,

Matthew 7:7,8
Ask and it will be given to you; seek and you will find;
knock and the door will be opened to you.
For everyone who asks receives; the one who seeks finds;
and to the one who knocks, the door will be opened.

How many times?

We should have set times during the day. But pray as often as possible.
As the need arises, prayers are to be a few times per day. There are
no length in time. Pray like your talking to your mother or your father
as if they are on the other side of the world. Trust me, He is listening
and taking notes of your conversations with Him.


Where at should you pray?

Jesus said that Prayer should be done in private. And not boasting in front
of others to make yourself feel better than they.

Matthew 6:6
But when you pray, go into your room, and when you have shut your door,
pray to your Father who is in the secret place;
and your Father who sees in secret will reward you openly.

So in conclusion. Pray as if your speaking to a loved one on the phone. As
if they were not in your precence. This will help you understand that even
though you cannot physically see God, He is always
with you no matter what. He is always listening. And is waiting until the rest of
the world is ready for his coming to gather you up in his arms. Like any
loving father would do after a long period of time.
