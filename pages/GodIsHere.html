This requires a much longer read to understand what is to take place
before his second coming.

But..

Please follow along with this so that you won't be caught off guard.

Matthew 24:3-5
And he sat upon the mount of Olives, the disciples came unto him privately,
saying, Tell us, when shall these things be? and what shall be the sign
of thy coming, and of the end of the world?

And Jesus answered and said unto them, Take heed that no man deceive you.

For many shall come in my name, saying, I am the Christ; and shall
deceive many.

So Jesus is telling us, do not let no one make you believe
in anything that he has not told you. Make sure your not deceived into believing
that God ( Jesus ) has returned because you'll surely know when he does as we continue.

Matthew 24:6-8
And ye shall hear of wars and rumors of wars: see that ye be not troubled:
for all these things must come to pass, but the end is not yet.


For nation shall rise against nation, and kingdom against kingdom: and there
shall be famines, and pestilences, and earthquakes, in divers places.
All these things are the beginning of sorrows.

So Jesus tells us that there will be wars and rumors of wars that will
come to pass. There will be massive food shortages (famines), there will
be many epidemic diseases (pestilences), and there will
be earthquakes around the world (divers places). This will be a sign of the
beginning of sorrows (Translated from Greek as oden meaning
"childbirth" because its an agonizing state to be in ).

Matthew 24:9-12
Then shall they deliver you up to be afflicted, and shall kill
you: and ye shall be hated of all nations for my name's sake.

And then shall many be offended, and shall betray one
another, and shall hate one another.

And many false prophets shall rise, and shall deceive many.

And because iniquity shall abound, the love of many shall wax cold

Here it is saying that as a believer in Jesus, you will be
burdened, persecuted, troubled by non-believers because you follow Jesus.
So then people will hate each other and be offended by one another because
you are a believer in Jesus Christ.

Many false people will come and go uttering divine prophecies and many will
choose to believe them as if they have divine knowledge. (prophets
translated from ancient Greek as pseudoprophetes meaning, people
who believe they have God's ability to foresee upcoming events.)

Then lastly he is stating that iniquity (violation/wickedness) shall abound (increase or multiply), the love of many will grow cold.


In Matthew 24:27 Jesus explains to his chosen group of people to
preserve his message, That as lightning comes out of the east, and shines
to the west, so it will be when He returns to earth.

In Matthew 24:29-31 Jesus is explaing; After those days, the sun will grow dark
and the moon will no longer shine. The stars will fall from heaven, and the
powers in heaven will be shaken. (possibly meteors falling onto earth, and large
blasts in space?)
Then there will be a sign in heaven (translated from ouranos meaning
the world, the universe), and the tribes of the earth will mourn, and we will see Jesus coming from the clouds with amazing power and glory.

Jesus in a very serious manner wanted to get a strong point across to his
chosen few. To make sure these words of his were understood. He told them this
parable(a story to illustrate a moral or spiritual lesson).

Matthew 24:43-44
But know this, that if the goodman of the house had known in what watch
the thief would come, he would have watched, and would not have suffered
his house to be broken up.
Therefore be ye also ready: for in such an hour as ye think not
the Son of man cometh.

Jesus is clearly telling us, look. I have been teaching you and giving you
so many hints. If you do not listen to me, I will show up back to earth
and you will be shocked and not ready for me just as you were not ready for
a theif.
Jesus gave us a book of instructions and it is up to all of us to believe his
words or when he shows up, it will be too late to change your mind. You'll
be left behind to suffer the earths ending.

All that has been said, there is much more so it is up to you to pick up
a Bible and read it. I recommend starting at the New testiment since we are
very close to the end times.
