What is God's Church?

Back to Home

Very simple. God's church is not in a church building.
It is not in any structure built on this entire planet. Gods church is...
Within you.
Each and everyone of us are churches according to Jesus.

Mat 16:18
And I say unto thee, That thou art Peter, and upon this rock
I will build my church; and the gates of hell shall not prevail against it.

Jesus actually gave Peter this nickname. Meaning "rock".
And Upon this rock, I will build my church.

The reference of the chruch is all over the book of Acts.

Act 14:27
And when they come, and had gathered the church
together, they rehearsed all that God had done with them
and how he had opened the door of faith unto the Gentiles.

This is saying when the apostles of Jesus gathered his followers
They told them all they learned about being saved. So that the Gentiles (non jewish people)
would also know how to be saved during the 2nd coming of Christ.

You see, God's church does not reside in a building or structure. God's messages
are his church.

If you do not attend church, it does not mean your not saved or your not a Christian.
You are a living breathing church so long as you spread the word of God.
