we feel. To die like a normal man, feeling the agnony of a human suffering.

to give us a new way of salvation.

Romans 10:9-10
That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus,
and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead,
thou shalt be saved.
For with the heart man believeth unto righteousness;
and with the mouth confession is made unto salvation.

Jesus is telling us that in order to be saved. You must
believe that he was sent here to give us the new way of salvation, even
if it meant that he would suffer in agonizing pain to do so.

before us in full glory, we would have no free will to choose.The time will
come when God will rule over us but the time is not right in his eyes.
We should never as a human species judge God's judgment. He is perfect.

something". They are speaking merely on man's opinion, not God's.
