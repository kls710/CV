What is sin?

Sin is a thought or action that we have as human beings.

God did not create us with sin. We were created in his
image. Meaning, we were given from day one the knowledge of right from wrong.
With free will comes the ability to sin. Otherwise we would all be robots
(and obviously God did not want robot children).

But..

Because we did disobey him, He still wanted to
teach us the way to be able to enter his kingdom of Heaven.

So sin is more-less doing the opposite of what our earthly parents do for us.
They make up rules, tell us what we shouldn't do, and they sit back to
see if we disobey their oders.

Same thing with God. He asks certain things of us and we should adhear to them.
But he strictly just wants us to believe him when he says that he came to
this earth to teach us his ways, and that he died on the cross trying to teach you his message.
